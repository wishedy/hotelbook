Hotel Introduction

The Country Comfort Mt Gambier International Hotel is located in the charming garden of 5 acres (2 hectares), featuring free WiFi and tennis courts. All air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV, a fridge, tea/coffee making facilities and en suite bathrooms with hairdryer and toiletries. The Country Comfort Mt Gambier International Hotel is a 5-minute drive from the town centre and a 10-minute drive from Blue Lake. Gambier Country Comfort International Hotel begins on May 27, 2013 on Booking.com chain hotel: Constellation Hotel
